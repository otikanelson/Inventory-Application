Delete Your Stockq Account

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your name, PIN, and login credentials
  • Store Information: Store name, address, and contact details
  • Inventory Data: All products, batches, and stock levels
  • Product Images: All uploaded product photos
  • Sales History: All sales transactions and records
  • AI Predictions: All prediction data and analytics
  • Alert Settings: Your custom alert configurations

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • All data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours
  • You will not be able to recover any data after deletion
  • You can create a new account anytime with the same or different credentials
ℹ️ Note: Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes for up to 90 days, including:
  • Transaction logs (for audit purposes)
  • Security logs (for fraud prevention)
This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Stockq app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to Account Management
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Enter your Security PIN to confirm
  6. Tap "Permanently Delete"

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

Email: your-email@example.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Stockq

Include in your email:

  • Your account name or store name
  • Your registered email (if provided)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Response Time: We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email.

Send Deletion Request Email

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Export Your Data: Download your inventory data before deletion (Settings → Data Management → Export Data)
  • Deactivate Instead: Temporarily deactivate your account if you might return later
  • Contact Support: If you're having issues, we're here to help
